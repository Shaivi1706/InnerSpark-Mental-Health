Our Features

Talk to Experts

Get personalized guidance from mental health professionals. Whether it’s a quick consultation or ongoing support, we’ve got your back.

Community Page

Join our supportive community of individuals on the same journey. Share experiences, ask questions, and connect with others in a safe space.

Track your mood

The "Track Your Mood" feature lets users select from four mood emojis, revealing tailored activity suggestions for each.

Music

Listen to calming music to help you unwind, meditate, or simply enjoy a peaceful break from the daily hustle.

SoulFlow Yoga

Our Yoga feature offers calming, guided sessions to help users relax and de-stress. Each session is tailored for mental clarity and inner peace, enhancing overall well-being.

Mira, Your AI Wellness Buddy

Mira offers personalized guidance for mental well-being, mood tracking, and self-care routines, all tailored to your unique needs. Whether you're looking for relaxation tips, mindfulness practices, or just a friendly chat, Mira is here to support you on your journey to inner peace.
