Dr. Sarah Blake

Dr. Sarah Blake is a highly experienced specialist in mental wellbeing with over 7 years of experience in treating depression, anxiety, and schizophrenia. She is dedicated to providing holistic care that not only addresses the symptoms but also the root causes of mental health challenges.

Specializations: Depression, Anxiety, Schizophrenia, Stress Management, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Qualifications

  • MD in Psychiatry, Harvard University
  • Certified Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) Practitioner
  • Licensed Mental Health Counselor (LMHC)
  • Member, American Psychological Association (APA)
  • Certified in Advanced Trauma Therapy

Experience

Dr. Sarah Blake has over 7 years of experience working with patients across diverse backgrounds. She has helped hundreds of patients overcome mental health challenges, particularly in the areas of depression, anxiety, and schizophrenia. She has worked in various settings, including private practices, hospitals, and counseling centers.
